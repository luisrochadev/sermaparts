Services

Custom Clearance

Licensed since 1991, we provide the most rapid clearance through customs, ensuring the delivery of time critical shipments

Our import specialists provide accurate tariff classifications for proper duty payments and compliance with other federal agencies.

We electronically file and monitor all shipments to provide accurate information to clients.

Logistics

Our warehosue facilities and services will allow you to store, handle, repack, consolidate, and ship your products at your convenience.

Transportation

Sermaparts provides full and partial loads to and from Mexico with multiple schedules from its base in San Diego, CA through our private fleet.

To ensure the best advantage for our customers, we constantly analyze and evaluate freight costs, cargo delivery times, and routes to recommend the best options for imports or exports.

Trade Consulting

In the customs research and consulting department, clients will recieve information related to international commerce and customs issues.

We deliver reliable, real-time information to guide our clients through the maze of complex and ever-changing regulations, thus ensuring compliance with all federal laws at the best rates.

Procurement Management

A key factor for our procurement services is meeting your business goals.

All purchasing decisions include factors such as delivery and handling, marginal benefit, and price fluctuations.

We will follow your instructions and those of your designers, contractors and purchase managers to provide you with the fastest and must cost effective process.

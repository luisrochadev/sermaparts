History

In 1991, Jose Luis Rocha and Claudia Salazar founded Rocha-Salazar Enterprises Incorporated in San Diego, CA in order to provide a new kind of freight-forwarding service; an integral "one stop" company that provides all of the logistics, import, and transportation needs to its customers capitalizing on Mexico's integration to GATT and the global economy.

2016 marks a milestone for us as we celebrate 25 years of offering the best professional services.

We proudly oversee over 20 employees, use the latest customs technology, maintain our in-house trucking fleet, and manage a storage capacity of over than 10,000 square feet.
